Support
帮助与支持

For account, order, privacy, or technical questions, contact the WebAZ support team. 如需账户、订单、隐私或技术帮助，请联系 WebAZ 支持团队。

contact@webaz.xyz

Do not send passwords, Passkey material, recovery codes, or payment-card details by email. 请勿通过邮件发送密码、Passkey 信息、恢复码或银行卡信息。

What to include / 建议提供的信息

  • Your WebAZ handle, if you have one / 你的 WebAZ 用户名（如有）
  • The order ID or product link, when relevant / 相关订单编号或商品链接
  • A short description of what happened and when / 问题现象及发生时间
  • Screenshots with private addresses, phone numbers, and payment details redacted / 已遮盖地址、电话和付款信息的截图

Order and payment help / 订单与付款

Direct Pay is a non-custodial buyer-to-seller payment method. WebAZ does not hold the transaction principal. For an active order, review the order page first for its current action, delivery, evidence, and dispute options. 直付为买卖双方直接付款，WebAZ 不托管交易本金；请先在订单页查看当前可执行操作、履约、举证与争议选项。

Privacy requests / 隐私请求

For access, correction, deletion, or privacy questions, email the same address and state that your request concerns privacy. See the full Privacy Policy.
