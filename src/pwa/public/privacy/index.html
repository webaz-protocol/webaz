Privacy Policy
隐私政策

Effective date: 2026-07-23 | 生效日期：2026-07-23

WebAZ is operated and published by XU FENGNA as an individual. WebAZ 由个人发布者 XU FENGNA 运营与发布。

This policy describes current WebAZ operational practices. It is not legal advice and does not claim external legal review. 本政策说明 WebAZ 当前的实际运营做法，不构成法律意见，也不声称经过外部法律审查。

1. What we collect / 我们收集什么

Account & identity / 账户与身份 — handle, display name, optional email and/or phone if you add them, and a coarse region for regulatory routing. WebAZ can be used with a minimal profile.

Shipping & fulfillment / 配送与履约 — addresses saved or attached to an order, including recipient, address text or components, and phone. This is needed to fulfill physical orders and is visible to the counterparty of that order, not publicly.

Authentication / 认证 — Passkey / WebAuthn credentials are stored as public keys only. Biometric data never leaves your device and is never sent to or stored by WebAZ.

Operational & security metadata / 运营与安全元数据 — connection IP and browser user-agent may be processed for active sessions, diagnostics, and rate limits. Registration, click-deduplication, and similar audit records use IP / user-agent hashes where raw values are not needed. Order and protocol audit trails and dispute records support fraud prevention and dispute resolution.

KYC / KYB and screening records / 身份与商户核验 — processed only when a regulated or risk-gated capability requires them, such as seller Direct Pay onboarding or a threshold withdrawal. They are not required for ordinary catalog browsing.

AI-assisted features / AI 辅助功能 — if you explicitly invoke one, the text or image needed for that request may be sent to the configured AI provider. Do not include unnecessary personal or confidential data.

2. Why we process it / 处理目的

We process this data to provide and secure the service, fulfill orders, prevent fraud and abuse, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations where applicable. Where a jurisdiction requires a legal basis, processing rests on contract performance, legitimate interests such as security and anti-fraud, and legal obligation.

3. How it is protected / 如何保护

  • No PII in the public record. The public dispute archive is PII-redacted; a live case is visible only to the parties and assigned arbitrator. 公开记录不含个人可识别信息。
  • Audit identifiers are hashed or anonymized where raw values are not needed. Limited raw session/security metadata may be retained as described above. PII is never intentionally placed in URLs or query strings.
  • Fund-moving and identity or PII-changing actions use human-presence Passkey gates and scoped agent permissions.

4. Sharing & third parties / 共享与第三方

We do not sell your data or share PII with third parties for marketing. Service processors used to operate WebAZ are:

  • Cloudflare — CDN, DNS, and email routing.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile — bot and abuse checks at sensitive entry points.
  • Railway — application and database hosting.
  • Anthropic, OpenAI, or a user-selected AI provider — only when an AI-assisted feature is invoked; the selected provider processes submitted prompt/content under its own terms.

Each processor handles data only to provide its function. We may disclose data when required by valid legal process, limited to what is required.

Connected clients / 已连接客户端 — if you connect WebAZ to ChatGPT or another agent client, that client receives the tool inputs and results needed to perform your request under the client's own privacy terms.

5. Retention / 留存

Account data is kept while an account is active. Operational and security metadata is retained for the fraud-prevention and audit window documented in protocol parameters. On account deletion, personal data is wiped except records that must be retained for legal or audit obligations, which remain redacted or aggregate where applicable.

6. Your rights / 你的权利

  • Access / export — export your orders and account data from the app.
  • Correction / 更正 — edit profile fields, addresses, and contact details yourself.
  • Deletion / 删除 — request account deletion in the app, subject to legal-retention exceptions.
  • Withdraw consent / 撤回同意 — the rewards and builder program is opt-in and can be turned off at any time.

People in GDPR, CCPA, and similar regimes have additional rights provided by those laws. WebAZ applies them where applicable.

7. Cookies & tracking / Cookie 与跟踪

WebAZ uses minimal session and authentication state plus the Turnstile bot check. It does not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

8. International handling / 跨境与司法管辖

WebAZ adapts to local-law requirements where applicable. Regional handling may differ when law requires tighter KYC, reporting, or data-protection controls.

9. Children / 未成年人

WebAZ is not directed to children under the age required by your jurisdiction, and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

10. Changes & contact / 变更与联系

Material changes are announced and, for the rewards program, re-consented through its consent-version mechanism. For questions or data requests, email contact@webaz.xyz.
